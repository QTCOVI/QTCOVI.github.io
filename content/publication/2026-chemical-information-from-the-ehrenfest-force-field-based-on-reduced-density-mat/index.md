Here we assess Reduced Density Matrix Functional Theory (RDMFT) as an alternative approach to approximate the exchange‐correlation contribution to the EhF field. Using a range of natural orbital functionals, we perform a topological analysis of the EhF and its exchange‐correlation component in representative molecules from simple diatomics to polyatomic systems.
