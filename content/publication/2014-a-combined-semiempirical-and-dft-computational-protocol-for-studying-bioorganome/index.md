abstract: "Computational methods can help in the design of new bioorganometallic compounds. However, the presence of multihapto or σ/π metal‐ligand bonding still precludes the direct application of either pure molecular mechanics (MM) or hybrid quantum mechanics‐MM methods to study the flexibility of biomolecules in complex with organometallics.
