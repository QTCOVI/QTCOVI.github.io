Ángel Martín Pendás is Professor of Physical Chemistry at the University of Oviedo and the founder of the QTCOVI group. His research focuses on developing orbital-invariant frameworks for understanding chemical bonding, most notably the Interacting Quantum Atoms (IQA) energy partition and the statistical analysis of the electron distribution in real space. He collaborates with groups across Europe, North America, and Latin America, and has contributed to widely used topological analysis codes including PROMOLDEN.
